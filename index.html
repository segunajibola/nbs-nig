Who We Are

About N.B.S NIG LIMITED

N.B.S NIG LIMITED is a Nigerian construction and general contracting company committed to delivering high-quality building and engineering solutions across residential, commercial, industrial, and public sector projects.

We combine technical expertise, skilled workmanship, and modern construction practices to deliver projects that meet the highest standards of quality, safety, and durability.

Whether constructing new buildings, renovating existing structures, or providing complete engineering services, we work closely with our clients to ensure every project is completed on time, within budget, and to specification.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and cost-effective construction solutions that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and professionalism.

Our Vision

To become one of Nigeria's most trusted construction companies, recognized for excellence, integrity, and sustainable project delivery.
